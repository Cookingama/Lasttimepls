Introduction

Cooking Ama is a visual novel game, that incorporates baking tips with a tragic story. This game includes the basic functions of Ren'Py, an interactive map, and the use of an inventory. The theme of this visual novel revolves around the bakery, it will give you the experience of creating your baked goods.

Frankie is a child who was abandoned by his parents. Luckily for Frankie, he was left in the hands of a well-known bakery store owner. He now has a new start to her life being an apprentice of a baker.
